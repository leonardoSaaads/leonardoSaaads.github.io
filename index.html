About me

My name is Leonardo, I am 23 years old and I graduated in the Interdisciplinary course in Sciences & Technology at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte from 2018 to 2022. During this period, I had access to extensive training in physics, mathematics, chemistry and provided with programming languages ​​like Python, C++ and Javascript.

In 2022 I became a telecommunications engineer, where I managed to get in touch with software such as GNU Radio and Ansys HFSS. Also, I worked on personal projects on Github to help the community and spread the knowledge. My goal is to make knowledge more accessible and dynamic so that other people can have their lives transformed - just as mine was.

I like to study and research areas related to Physics and Mathematics modeling. In addition, I intend to develop new techniques and skills regarding Deep Learning, Cloud Computing, Big Data and Telecom softwares.
